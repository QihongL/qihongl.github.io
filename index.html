Welcome to my personal webpage!

Hi, I'm Qihong, or just "Q", an undergrad studying psychology and math at the U. of Wisconsin-Madison. Combining behavioral experiments, neuroimaging, and computational modeling, I have been studying semantic knowledge representation and mathematical cognition, advised by Professor Tim Rogers, Maryellen MacDonald, Jay McClelland and others. Their dedicated guidance had a significant influence on me. I deeply appreciate their support. Outside the lab, I enjoy watching Japanese anime, tasting desserts and learning violin.
