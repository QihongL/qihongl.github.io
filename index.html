Welcome to my personal webpage.

Hi, I'm Qihong, or "Q" for convenience. I am currently an undergraduate student studying psychology and mathematics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I want to understand how the brain, and human cognition, works in general. And I am particularly interested in the neural representations of concepts, knowledge and memory. During my undergraduate career, I have been extremely fortunate to work with Professor Maryellen MacDonald, Tim Rogers and Jay McClelland and others. I deeply appreciate everyone's guidance and support.

I plan to apply for interdisciplinary Ph.D. programs in human cognition this fall (2016).
